Introduction

Hello! I'm a second year student studying Cognitive Science and East Asian Studies at the University of Toronto. I usually go by CursedBlackCat online, but most people call me Cat or CBC for short. Which is actually an unintentional side effect of my choice of online handle, as I am indeed a CBC (Canadian Born Chinese)! Some circles I'm in, most notably the Tower of Saviors community, know me better by my old handle, TheCatGod. I go by the pronouns he/him. I can speak English and Cantonese fluently, and I can get by in French and Japanese as well.
